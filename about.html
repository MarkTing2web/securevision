The Founder's Story

Why Ler Wee Meng Started Securevision

Most security companies are started by salespeople. Securevision was started by an engineer with a law degree — and that difference shapes everything we do.

After more than a decade working across Singapore's security systems industry, our founder Ler Wee Meng saw the same problem repeating: security systems that detected problems but couldn't tell you what was wrong. An alarm fires. Someone gets a call. But nobody knows if it's a break-in, a sensor fault, or a cat crossing the yard.

He called it the "Blind Alarm Problem" — and in 2006, he founded Securevision specifically to solve it. The approach was different from the start: assess the site like an engineer, design the system like an architect, and stand behind it like a professional with something to lose.

His engineering background means every system we design is specified for how it actually needs to work — not what's easiest to install. His legal background means we understand the regulatory framework, the liability implications, and the compliance requirements our clients operate under.

  • Every system proposal is signed off by a qualified engineer — not a sales rep
  • We hold a Police Licence under the Private Security Industry Act — legally required, not optional
  • Our contracts are clear on what we deliver, when, and what happens if we don't
  • We answer the phone after handover — because our reputation depends on it

Our Approach

The Securevision Philosophy

Three principles that have governed every project since 2006 — and the reason clients come back, and refer others.

🔬

Engineer First, Sell Second

Every engagement starts with a site assessment and risk analysis — not a product catalogue. We specify what your property actually needs, even if that means recommending a smaller scope than you expected.

ðŸ”—

Systems, Not Products

A camera that doesn't talk to your access control isn't a security system — it's surveillance theatre. We design systems where every component serves a defined role and integrates with every other. No silos. No gaps.

📞

Accountable After Handover

We answer the phone. Maintenance contracts, spare parts stocked, rapid response. The system we install is one we're prepared to maintain for the next decade — which means we install it properly the first time.

⚖️

Legal & Regulatory Clarity

Our founder's legal background means we understand the compliance obligations our clients operate under — PDPA, MAS TRM, BCA requirements, and SPF regulations. We design systems that hold up to scrutiny.

💰

You Own Everything

No subscription lock-ins. No proprietary cloud you lose access to if you stop paying. Every system we install is fully owned by you — hardware, software licences, and documentation included.

Right-Sized for Your Property

A private home doesn't need an enterprise platform. An industrial facility doesn't need a residential intercom. We match the complexity and cost of the system to what your property genuinely requires.

We carry 20 brands across five system categories. That's a deliberate decision — not a catalogue. Every brand we list is one we've evaluated, installed at scale, and are prepared to maintain and warrant.
