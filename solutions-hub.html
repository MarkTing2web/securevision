Why Property Type Determines Your Security Approach

Access Patterns

  • Residential: 4-6 people, predictable
  • Condo: 1,500 residents, high variance
  • Commercial: Staff + customers, 9am-6pm
  • Industrial: Shift workers, 24/7 logistics
  • Institutional: Public + controlled zones

Threat Profile

  • Residential: Burglary, vandalism
  • Condo: Tailgating, unauthorized parking
  • Commercial: Shoplifting, after-hours break-ins
  • Industrial: Cargo theft, safety incidents
  • Institutional: Intrusion, compliance violations

System Requirements

  • Residential: Mobile control, easy DIY
  • Condo: Scalable, low guard dependency
  • Commercial: Multi-site monitoring, AI alerts
  • Industrial: Integration with ERP/logistics
  • Institutional: MHA/MOE compliance, audit trails
