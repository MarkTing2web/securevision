The Challenge

A 450-Unit Estate Running on Fragmented Legacy Systems

The L'viv MCST inherited a patchwork of aging security systems — separate vendors for CCTV, intercom, and access control, none of which communicated. Residents were frustrated with unreliable intercoms and manual gate logbooks. Security staff had no unified view of the estate.

  • Analog cameras with poor night coverage across carpark levels
  • Manual visitor logbook at guardhouse — creating queues and gaps
  • Residents had to call guardhouse to admit visitors — high call volume
  • 3 separate vendor contracts — no single point of accountability
  • No mobile management — MCST chair had no visibility after hours
  • Aging boom gate system with frequent mechanical failures
The Solution

One Platform. Every System. Full Estate Control.

Securevision designed a complete estate security infrastructure under the SECURE™ methodology — replacing all four legacy systems with integrated, VESTA™-managed subsystems and deploying GantryGo™ for resident-controlled visitor access.

  • 4K IP cameras across all zones — lobbies, carpark, perimeter, lifts
  • VESTA™ resident app — visitors registered via QR pre-booking
  • GantryGo™ intercom — residents release barrier from smartphone
  • UHF windscreen tags for seamless resident vehicle entry
  • Biometric + card turnstiles at all lift lobbies
  • Single management dashboard for MCST and security staff
