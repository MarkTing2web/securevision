Why It Matters

Vehicle Access Done Right

A boom gate by itself isn't a vehicle access system. When ANPR reads the plate, the barrier opens automatically, and the event is logged with a camera snapshot — that's a vehicle access system.

Securevision designs vehicle access infrastructure that eliminates manual guard intervention, prevents tailgating, and gives management a complete log of every vehicle movement in and out of the property.

  • ANPR recognition accuracy 99%+ in Singapore conditions
  • UHF long-range tags for seamless resident access (no stopping)
  • Tailgating detection via intercom, sensors, and camera integration
  • Complete vehicle movement log for audit and investigation
  • Integration with people access for linked resident profiles
  • GantryGo™ intercom for visitor authorisation and emergency override

Securevision Proprietary

GantryGo™ Visitor Intercom System

GantryGo™ is Securevision's purpose-built gantry intercom platform — designed specifically for the visitor management challenges unique to Singapore condominiums and gated properties.

When a visitor arrives, they call the resident via the gantry intercom. The resident sees a live video feed and can open the barrier directly from their smartphone — from anywhere. Every visitor call is logged with a photo and timestamp.

  • No security guard intervention required for resident-authorised visitors
  • Pre-booking allows automatic barrier release for expected visitors
  • Management override for emergencies and contractor access
  • Integrates with ANPR for combined plate + intercom verification
