Why It Matters

Vehicle Access Done Right

A boom gate by itself isn't a vehicle access system. When ANPR reads the plate, the barrier opens automatically, and the event is logged with a camera snapshot — that's a vehicle access system.

Securevision designs vehicle access infrastructure that eliminates manual guard intervention, prevents tailgating, and gives management a complete log of every vehicle movement in and out of the property.

  • ANPR recognition accuracy 99%+ in Singapore conditions
  • UHF long-range tags for seamless resident access (no stopping)
  • Tailgating detection via intercom, sensors, and camera integration
  • Complete vehicle movement log for audit and investigation
  • Integration with people access for linked resident profiles
  • GantryGo™ intercom for visitor authorisation and emergency override

Vehicle Systems

From Boom Gates to Road Blockers

We specify the right barrier technology for each entry point — balancing throughput, security level, and operational requirements.

🚧

Boom Gates & Barriers

High-cycle boom gates for residential and commercial car parks. Rapid open/close times reduce queue buildup at peak hours.

  • 1.5 to 6m boom lengths
  • High-cycle motors (10M+ cycles)
  • Anti-crash safety loops
  • LED illuminated boom option
  • Manual hand crank override
🔍

ANPR / LPR Systems

Automatic Number Plate Recognition with AI-powered character detection. Handles partial plates, dirty plates, and varied lighting conditions.

  • Day & night operation
  • SG plate format optimised
  • Blacklist / whitelist logic
  • Snapshot with every event
  • Cloud or on-premise database
📻

UHF Long-Range Systems

Radio-frequency tags on windscreens for seamless resident access without stopping or winding down windows. Ideal for condominiums and gated housing.

  • Up to 10m read distance
  • No stopping required
  • Tag registration + management
  • Visitor UHF temporary tags
  • Anti-clone encrypted tags
⛔

Road Blockers & Bollards

High-security hostile vehicle mitigation for government, institutional, and critical infrastructure sites. Crash-rated to PAS 68/IWA 14.

  • Automatic rising bollards
  • Road blockers (crash-rated)
  • Tyre killers (anti-reversal)
  • Hydraulic & electromechanical
  • Safety presence sensors
🅿️

Parking Management Systems

Integrated parking guidance, bay counting, and payment management. Real-time availability display reduces internal traffic and frustration.

  • Bay-level occupancy sensors
  • LED guidance displays
  • Cashless payment terminals
  • Mobile parking reservation
  • Reporting & analytics dashboard

Securevision Proprietary

GantryGo™ Visitor Intercom System

GantryGo™ is Securevision's purpose-built gantry intercom platform — designed specifically for the visitor management challenges unique to Singapore condominiums and gated properties.

When a visitor arrives, they call the resident via the gantry intercom. The resident sees a live video feed and can open the barrier directly from their smartphone — from anywhere. Every visitor call is logged with a photo and timestamp.

  • No security guard intervention required for resident-authorised visitors
  • Pre-booking allows automatic barrier release for expected visitors
  • Management override for emergencies and contractor access
  • Integrates with ANPR for combined plate + intercom verification

Applications

Vehicle Access for Every Property Type

Condominium

Resident & Visitor Car Park

UHF tags for seamless resident access, ANPR for visitor management, GantryGo™ intercom for resident-authorised entry. Complete movement log for MCST reporting.

Commercial

Office Building Car Park

ANPR-integrated boom gates with cashless payment. Tenant allocation management — reserved bays, visitor bays, and loading bay time limits all configured centrally.

Industrial

Logistics Hub & Warehouse

Fleet whitelist for authorised vehicles, contractor plate pre-registration, and blacklist alerts for unauthorised vehicles. Dock scheduling integration for logistics operations.

Gated Housing

Private Estate & Bungalow

Fully automated resident entry with UHF, visitor intercom with video, and barrier integration. Managed from a central guardhouse or entirely guardless with mobile oversight.

Institution

School & Government Facility

Whitelist-only access during school hours, parent pick-up management, and contractor access scheduling. Road blockers for high-security perimeters.

Mixed-Use

Integrated Development

Separate access domains for residential, retail, and office tenants sharing a car park. Revenue-managed public bays alongside resident-reserved areas.
